Japanese Chicken Curry

Recipe source

Description

Japanese Chicken Curry is a variation of Indian curry brought to Japan by the British. Japanese Chicken Curry leans more on the sweet side but can be made spicier. It consists of chicken, yellow onion, potatoes and carrots. The dish is usually paired with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 ginger root
  • 680g boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • pepper, to taste

Curry Sauce

  • neutral oil
  • 960ml chicken stock/water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Japanese curry roux cubes ie. Golden Curry, Vermont Curry, etc.
  • pepper, to taste
