Description

Tomato egg scramble is a simple dish consisting of only four ingredients. This is an ideal breakfast for someone looking to add a healthy twist to a classic dish.

Ingredients

  • neutral oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tomatoes, minced
  • salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat pan on medium-low heat
  2. Add oil to pan once the pan is hot
  3. Crack eggs in a bowl. Mix well with a fork
  4. Add the minced tomatoes to the egg mixture
  5. Slowly add egg mixture to the pan.
  6. Constantly stir the egg mixture inside the pan to create a fluffy texture. Cook until the eggs are no longer runny. Enjoy
